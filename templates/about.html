About Om Mills

Om Mills is a 4 years 8 months old Proprietorship Firm incorporated on 30-May-2019, having its registered office located at S F No.233/2, Vettukattuvalasu, Saiva Maariamman Kovil Road, Erode, Tamil Nadu.

The major activity of Om Mills is Manufacturing, Sub-classified into Manufacture of textiles and is primarily engaged in the Weaving manufacture of cotton and cotton mixture fabrics.

Om Mills is classified as Micro enterprise in the financial year 2020-21. It has its unit situated at Erode, Tamil Nadu.
